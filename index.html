this portfolio is focused on american student loan debt and its thorny dimensions, based around federal student loan data.

there are three different components: a visual component, an interactive fiction piece, and an analog art project. the visual component is a series of generated data visualizations, while the interactive fiction piece is a choice-based narrative putting the reader in the shoes of a incoming college freshman today. the analog art project takes the consequences of students taking on loans and fuses them with the trappings of college graduation.

(everything you see on this website has been hand-coded. enjoy.)
